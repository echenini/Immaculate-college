our services

    Goshen College offers a range of services to support students, faculty, and staff. Here are some of the key services available:

  • VALUES

    Anabaptist heritage emphasizing peace, justice, and compassion.

  • Academic Programs:

    Goshen College provides nearly 40 undergraduate majors and minors, as well as graduate programs, including a Master of Arts in Environmental Education, Master of Science in Nursing, and Master of Business Administration.

  • Study Abroad:

    The college has a strong study abroad program, with semester-long programs in countries like Ecuador, Indonesia, Senegal, and Tanzania. Approximately 65% of students participate in study abroad programs.

  • Sustainability Initiatives:

    The college prioritizes sustainability, with initiatives like renewable energy credits, energy-efficient buildings, and a food composting system.
